# 🎫 BotTicKet

Professional Discord ticket and sales management bot developed by **Polat Studio**.

The bot streamlines customer support, sales operations, and ticket management through an advanced interactive system built for modern Discord communities.

---

## ✨ Features

- Interactive modal-based ticket system
- Custom support & sales categories
- Fully in-Discord management dashboard
- Automated billing & payment workflows
- Real-time price and tax calculations
- Smart response & delay notifications
- Professional staff rating system
- Analytics & performance leaderboards
- Full ticket transcripts & archiving
- Modern UI optimized for support communities
